Event Venue

The concert will be held at the Open Air Amphitheater, located at 123 Music Lane, Melody City.

Transportation:

Free parking is available on site. Public transportation is also available via the Melody City Metro.

Accommodations:

Nearby hotels include The Music Inn and Harmony Suites, both offering special rates for concert attendees.
